Scalp scoring

20 points at play each game. The winner takes the opponent's scalp, the loser gets nothing. The split depends on the FargoRate gap:

Rating gapPoints (fav – dog)
0–3010 – 10

Bonus: Break & Run +4, Table Run +2 — enter BR/TR in the BCAPL app.

Key rules

  • Ladder points: Win 5 · Draw 3 · Loss 1 · Loss but leading at post 2 · Win by forfeit 6 · Marquee win +1.
  • Format: 8-ball & 10-ball, singles + Scotch Doubles, finishing with a Marquee matchup between each team's top-rated player. Up to 7 frames per player a night.
  • Attire: approved Diamond League jersey or League T-shirt required — no shirt, you forfeit your 10-ball game. Brand-new players who don't own one yet are the only exception, and must buy one ASAP.
  • Running late: 10 minutes' grace, then the frame is forfeited. Weekly $90 team fee is paid before play.
  • Talk & time-outs: two team time-outs per match; discussions capped at 40 seconds; no sideline coaching. A 40-sec shot clock may be enforced if play drags (never on Marquee).
  • Team cap: the three playing must total under 1775 FargoRate. Fill-ins must be within 35 Fargo of who they replace; no fill-ins in Finals.
  • Finals: top 16 on the Grand Ladder reach the Las Vegas Showdown at Club9. Need 6+ matches played to be eligible (8+ if unrated). Marquee players can't be swapped.
  • Scores: only captains or officials submit results in the BCAPL app. Score manipulation is serious misconduct.
