Key rules at a glance

  • Ladder points: Win 5 · Draw 3 · Loss 1 · Loss but leading at post 2 · Win by forfeit 6 · Marquee win +1.
  • Attire: approved Diamond League jersey or League T-shirt is required — turn up without it and you forfeit your 10-ball game.
  • Running late: 10 minutes' grace, then the frame is forfeited (recorded as a WF). Weekly $90 team fee is paid before play.
  • Time-outs & talk: two team time-outs per match; any discussion capped at 40 seconds; no sideline coaching. A 40-sec shot clock can be enforced if play drags (never on Marquee).
  • Fill-ins: must be within 35 FargoRate of the player replaced and keep the team under cap. No fill-ins allowed in Finals.
  • Finals: top 16 on the Grand Ladder make the Las Vegas Showdown at Club9. You need 6+ matches played to be eligible (8+ if unrated). Marquee players can't be swapped out.
  • Scores: only captains or officials submit results in the BCAPL Scoring App. Score manipulation is serious misconduct.
